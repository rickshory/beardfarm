I have a communal house here in Portland. People call it the "Beard Farm". Why?

Sherlock Holmes never said "Elementary my dear Watson". Leo Durocher never said "Nice guys finish last". I never called my house the "Beard Farm". But sometimes things just click, and you've got to go with it.

Calling it a "farm" is like saying it takes some effort to grow beards. No. Beards grow on their own. All the effort is in shaving!
